About GTA Somalia

Grand Theft Auto Somalia (GTA Somalia) is an action game where you will rob, kill, fight for survival and even steal wheelbarrows!
You are able to play as 2 diffrent playable characters both with unique skill sets. Waabberi a born midget has the skill set for melee and heavy weapons,
while Awaale is more focused on light weapons and is a better driver as his hands have better grip on the wheelbarrows!
A bit of Story
The two brothers from diffrent mothers Waabberi and Awaale are tired of working with other Somalian pirates and want to make a name for themselfs
so they leave but then the pirates get angry and go after the 2 brothers! After the brothers escape they run into more problems.
